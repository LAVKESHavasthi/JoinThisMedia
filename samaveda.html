Samaveda

The Samaveda is the "Veda of Chants," focusing on the melodic rendering of Rigvedic hymns for rituals.

Key Features

The Samaveda consists of verses primarily derived from the Rigveda, arranged for musical and ceremonial purposes.

Significance

The Samaveda plays a crucial role in the evolution of Indian classical music and maintains the connection between spirituality and melody.
